Homemade Meatball Recipe

Description

This quick and easy recipe will leave you with a stockpile of meatballs worth their weight in gold. This is a family recipe that has been around for at least one generation, so I am completely biased in saying that these are the finest meatballs I have ever tasted. The recipe is pretty straightforward, so good luck even though you won't need it. Additionally, If you do go the "chef mike" route, I would heavily suggest that you leave enough space between the individual meatballs in the casserole dish.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 pound ground beef
  • 1/4 pound ground pork
  • 1 cup fine dry bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 teaspoon parsley, minced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients and a large mixing bowl.
  2. Incorporate all ingredients inside of the bowl and mix until homogenous.
  3. Roll meatballs to your desired size and place them in a casserole dish.
  4. Either cover the casserole dish and cook in the microwave(trust me) or cook the meatballs in a pot of tomato sauce. Cook until they reach an internal temperature of 155 degrees Fahrenheit.
