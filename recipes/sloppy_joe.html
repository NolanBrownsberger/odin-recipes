Sloppy Joe Recipe

Description

As delicious as it looks disgusting, this brown sludge will teach you not to judge a book by its cover. Best served over rice, noodles, or on a bun, this flavorful combination of beef, beans, and veggies creates a taste you won't soon forget. Put on your apron and prepare for a quick and easy recipe that will have you coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (16 ounce) can baked beans, halfway-drained
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can tomato soup
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons mustard

Steps

  1. Gather and prepare the various ingredients.
  2. Start by cooking the ground beef over medium heat in a deep frying pan with the chopped bell pepper, onion, and minced garlic.
  3. Once the meat has browned, stir the halfway-drained can of baked beans, tomato soup, ketchup, and mustard into the dish.
  4. After a few minutes have passed, reduce the heat to a simmer and cover the pan. Continue cooking for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Now that the dish has thickened, it is complete. Consider serving the mixture over noodles, rice, or on a bun. Enjoy!
